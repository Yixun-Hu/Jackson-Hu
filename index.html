Embrace Every Possibility of the Future


一个勇士，在决斗之前也会向上帝祈祷，祈求上帝赐予他勇气与决断，但他从来不会祈求胜利本身。在我看来，祈求胜利本身是一种懦夫的行为，因为这意味着你缺乏足够的勇气来面对未知的未来。

Before a duel, a true warrior also turns to God in prayer, asking for the gift of courage and resolve, but never does he pray for victory itself. In my perspective, requesting victory alone is an act of cowardice, suggesting an absence of the bravery required to face an uncertain future.
